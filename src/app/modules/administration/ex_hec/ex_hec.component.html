Ex-Hall Executive Committee

The Ex-Hall Executive Committee (Ex-HEC) comprises former student leaders who previously served on the Hall Executive Committee (HEC), overseeing governance and management. With rich experience, they contribute to policy, infrastructure, and resident welfare. They mentor current HEC members, preserving institutional knowledge and guiding critical decisions. Their pride stems from shaping the hall's culture and environment positively. Though their official duties ended, their commitment to the hall's success persists, advocating for its prosperity and growth.
